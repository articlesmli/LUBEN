Lucid and Universal Bioapplicability Evaluation of Nanoformulations (LUBEN)

{% load static %}

This initiative focuses on the development of a computational framework designed to quantify the functional changes in small biomolecules when they are conjugated with nanoparticles. Tracking this impact in different environments and time frames would provide information for nanoparticle portfolio generation and support nanomedicine research, respectively. The approach has the potential to be developed for large biomolecules. Attention: The project is Under Development.
